My research focusses on the development of computational models for multiscale phenomena and related structural optimization applications. Specifically, I develop efficient optimization algorithms and large scale simulation techniques to improve the macroscopic structural response. I primarily use topology optimization and finite element analysis in achieving various design objectives. Recent focus has been on microstructure modeling with nonlinear material response tailored for impact loading and stress wave mitigation.

Research Interests: Computational mechanics, Structural optimization, Finite elements, Multiscale modeling, Continuum mechanics
