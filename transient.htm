In shape optimization, a typical design problem is posed as finding the optimal shape of a structure under a given loading so as to minimize a cost function subject to certain constraints. As an example, finding an optimal shape of a cantilever beam to minimize the tip deflection subject to volume constraint. Methods like shape optimization work on a subset of allowable shapes with fixed topological properties, that is no new boundaries (ex: holes) can be introduced.
